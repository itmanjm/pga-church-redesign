About Us

A Community of Faith

Portmore Gospel Assembly is a community of persons committed to God and each other in Worship, Edification, Evangelization and Spiritual Warfare, for the purpose of reconciling and discipling the nations unto Christlikeness.

Led by Pastor Donovan D. Cole since 1994, we are an Assemblies of God Affiliated church serving the Portmore community for over 30 years.
